% Atlas ROIs derived from the Talairach Daemon (www.talairach.org) developed by Jack Lancaster and Peter Fox at the Research Imaging Center of the University of Texas Health Science Center San Antonio (UTHSCSA).
%
% The script try01.m in this folder generates the following simplified atlas ROI files:
% Td.img in conn/roi folder corresponds to TDf below
%

TDa - Talairach areas,  Gyrus-level                     (55 areas)
        example: Angular Gyrus
TDb - Talairach areas,  Gyrus*Hemisphere-level          (127 areas)
        example: Angular Gyrus.Left Cerebrum
TDc - Talairach areas,  Cell-level                      (42 areas)
        example: Brodmann area 10
TDd - Talairach areas,  Cell*Hemisphere-level           (84 areas)
        example: Brodmann area 10.Left Cerebrum
TDe - Talairach areas,  Gyrus-level,Cerebrum only       (35 areas)
        example: Angular Gyrus
TDf - Talairach areas,  Gyrus*Hemisphere-level,Cerebrum only (70 areas)
        example: Angular Gyrus.Left Cerebrum
TDg - Talairach areas,  Lobe-level (8 areas)
        example: Temporal Lobe
